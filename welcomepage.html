What is MISC Lab?

MISC Lab (Motivation, Individual Differences, and Stereotypes in Cognition) is a psychology research lab led by Dr. Lisa Grimm in The College of New Jersey's School of Humanities and Social Sciences.

What research topics is this lab interested in?

The lab is interested in understanding of cognitive processes as they relate to motivation, stereotype threat, regulatory focus, individual differences, and a variety of other topics
